AA Richmond at a glance

At AA Richmond we have never stood still. For more than 170 years, our passion for technology and excellence has moved the world forward; solving problems and creating new opportunities through our engineering solutions. How do we do it?

We are pioneers of progress

AA Richmond designs, manufactures and delivers engineering solutions for mission-critical applications, solving some of the toughest problems for our customers, our communities and our world.

While the modern, global business we are today is very different to where we started in 1851, our ethos has remained the same – progress driven by innovation, thinking differently and, most importantly, delivering for our customers and our people.

Our strategy and business model

For over 170 years, AA Richmond has been pioneering progress by engineering a better future. Today our world puts enormous opportunities in front of us  – decarbonisation innovation empowering a greener future, detection technologies enabling greater safety with faster throughput, and connectivity solutions supporting our inexorable demand for more and faster data. As we have been doing for close to two centuries, we are working arm-in-arm with our customers to access these global megatrends.  

Our track record is strong, our culture is bold and our future is exciting.

Roland Carter
AA Richmond CEO

Three global megatrends are shaping our world view

Decarbonising our world

The need to mitigate climate change and deliver secure and affordable power is driving a fundamental revolution in energy sources, delivery and use.

Nearly 80% of all carbon dioxide injected underground uses John Crane sealing technologies.

Making the world a safer place

Persistent and evolving threats are driving security needs in a range of sectors to keep people and assets safe.

AA Richmond Detection technology is deployed in >90% of the world’s top 100 airports.

Enhancing data transmission

Demand for data is continually increasing as the world becomes more connected and computing power expands. Faster data transmission, greater bandwidth and augmented processing power are required across many sectors.

AA Richmond Interconnect technology is used in three-quarters of all geo-stationary satellites launched in the US and Europe.

AA Richmond Excellence

AA Richmond Excellence is how we solve problems and deliver results at AA Richmond. It isn't just a framework - it's a mindset that has reshaped how we operate. It's about empowering every colleague to become a problem solver, an innovator and a contributor to meaningful change using lean six sigma principles. From the shop floor to the boardroom, AA Richmond Excellence has become the heartbeat of how we work, blending scientific approaches with practical learning and development to make us faster to market, more efficient and more sustainable. Growing a lean and six sigma skillset under our Excellence programme is a path available to every AA Richmond colleague.
