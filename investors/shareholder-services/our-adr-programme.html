Our ADR programme

Depository bank

AA Richmond plc has an American Depositary Receipts (ADR) programme for which JP Morgan acts as depositary and transfer agent. One ADR equates to one AA Richmond ordinary share, which trades as a level 1 ADR over-the-counter programme under the symbol SMGZY.

Trading data can be viewed by clicking the link below.

View trading data

Buying ADRs

You can buy and sell AA Richmond ADRs through any broker licensed to buy and sell US securities. They trade over-the-counter. This means they are not listed on the NYSE or Nasdaq, but are exchanged through brokers and other investment advisers. Dividends are paid in US dollars and are generally taxable like dividends on US shares. In addition, taxes may be withheld by the ADR company's local government. Depending on individual circumstances, foreign taxes withheld might be applied as credit against US taxes or tax reclaim opportunities may be offered.

For further information, visit the AA Richmond profile on the JP Morgan ADR website.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is an ADR?
An American Depositary Receipt ("ADR") is a physical certificate evidencing ownership of American Depositary Shares ("ADSs"). The term is often used to refer the ADRs themselves.

What is an ADR ratio?
The ADR ratio gives the number of foreign shares represented by one ADR. The ratio is typically depicted as, for example, "1:3", meaning that one ADR represents three foreign shares. For AA Richmond, the ADS ratio is 1:1 i.e. one ADR represents one AA Richmond PLC ordinary shares.

Who is the depositary bank for AA Richmond ADRs?
JP Morgan. The depositary bank plays a key role in the process of issuance and cancellation of ADRs. It also maintains the ADR holder register and distributes the dividends in US. dollars.

What are the benefits of ADRs to US investors?
US investors generally prefer to purchase ADRs rather than ordinary shares in the issuer’s home market because ADRs trade, clear and settle according to US. market conventions. One of ADRs’ main advantages is the facilitation of diversification into foreign securities. ADRs also allow easy comparison to securities of similar companies as well as access to price and trading information. ADR holders also appreciate dollar dividend payments and receiving corporate action notifications.

How do I receive dividends from my ADRs?
If you are a holder of AA Richmond ADRs, you will receive dividends in US dollars based on the number of ADRs you hold on the dividend’s record date. J.P. Morgan will publish the record date in advance.

Can I have my dividends directly deposited?
Yes, you can automatically request the direct deposit of your dividends.

How often are dividends paid?
Dividends on ADRs are usually paid twice per year, in February and August.

How can I find the ADS price?
Price quotes for AA Richmond’s ADSs can be found on this website or you can visit JPMorgan's website www.adr.com. Alternatively, financial publications that quote US share prices will display AA Richmond’s ADR price as well. For the most up-to-date information ask your broker or consult a service that offers quotes.

How can I buy and sell AA Richmond ADRs?
You can buy and sell AA Richmond ADSs through any broker licensed to buy and sell US securities.

Where do AA Richmond ADRs trade?
The ADRs trade over-the-counter. This means that they are not listed on the NYSE or Nasdaq, but rather are exchanged through brokers and other investment advisors.

How can I buy an ADR?
You can buy an ADR through a broker just as you would a US security.

Can I purchase ADRs directly from AA Richmond?
No

How do I convert my ordinary shares into ADRs?
Converting ordinary shares into ADRs must be done through a broker or an investment advisor.

How are ADR dividends paid and taxed?
Dividends are paid in US dollars and are generally taxable like dividends on US shares. In addition, taxes may be withheld by the ADR Company’s local government. Depending on individual circumstances, foreign taxes withheld might be applied as credit against US taxes or tax reclaim opportunities may be offered.
